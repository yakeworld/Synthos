# GB/T 7714-2015 Reference Formatting Guide

> For Chinese science project proposals (NSFC, provincial, municipal)

## Journal Article Template

```
[#] Author1 A, Author2 B, Author3 C, et al. Title of article[J]. Journal Name, Year, Volume(Issue): Pages.
```

### Rules

| Element | Rule | Example |
|:--------|:-----|:--------|
| **Authors** | Surname first, initials after. 3 authors or fewer: list all. 4+: list first 3 + "et al." | Fan Y-H, Wu P-Y, Lin C-Z, et al. |
| **Title** | Sentence case. Capitalize first word and proper nouns only. End with period. | Predicting the probability of the incidence of maxillary sinus fungus ball in patients using nomogram models. |
| **[J] tag** | Journal article type marker — always present for journal papers | [J] |
| **Journal name** | Full name preferred for Chinese proposals (not abbreviated). End with comma. | Diagnostics, |
| **Year** | 4 digits, comma after | 2023, |
| **Volume** | Number only, no "Vol." prefix | 13 |
| **(Issue)** | In parentheses if present. No comma after volume if issue exists. | (19) |
| **Separator** | Colon before page numbers | : |
| **Pages** | First-last page numbers. Regular hyphen (-), NOT en-dash (–). | 3156. |

### Examples

**Correct:**
```
[1] Fan Y-H, Wu P-Y, Lin C-Z, et al. Predicting the probability of the incidence of maxillary sinus fungus ball in patients using nomogram models[J]. Diagnostics, 2023, 13(19): 3156.
[2] Cha H, Kim D, Lee S, et al. Clinical characteristics other than intralesional hyperdensity may increase the preoperative diagnostic accuracy of maxillary sinus fungal ball[J]. Clinical and Experimental Otorhinolaryngology, 2020, 13(3): 300-306.
[3] Thompson G R, Patterson T F. Fungal disease of the nose and paranasal sinuses[J]. Journal of Allergy and Clinical Immunology, 2024, 153(4): 811-822.
```

**Common errors to avoid:**

| Error | Wrong | Correct |
|:------|:------|:--------|
| No [J] tag | Fan et al. Diagnostics, 2023… | Fan et al.[J]. Diagnostics, 2023… |
| En-dash in pages | 300–306 | 300-306 |
| Abbreviated journal name | N Engl J Med | New England Journal of Medicine |
| Missing issue number | 13: 3156 | 13(19): 3156 |
| Missing "et al." for 4+ authors | Author A, Author B, Author C, Author D… | Author A, Author B, Author C, et al. |
| Period after title missing | Predicting…nomogram models[J] | Predicting…nomogram models[J]. |
