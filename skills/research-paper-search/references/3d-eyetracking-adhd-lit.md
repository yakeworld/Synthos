## Research Gap Analysis

**No existing study uses**: head-mounted 3D eye tracking + natural scene interaction for ADHD multimodal intelligent screening.

**Current state**: Most studies use stationary eye trackers or VR environments. None combine:
- Wearable head-mounted 3D eye tracking
- Natural scene (not VR/simulator) interaction
- Multimodal (eye tracking + EEG + facial expression + etc.)
- Clinical screening for ADHD

**This is the NSFC project's innovation space.**
