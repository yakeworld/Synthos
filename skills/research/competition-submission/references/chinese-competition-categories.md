# Chinese Competition Professional Category Mapping

For Chinese medical education and AI-for-medicine competitions, the "专业大类" (professional category) field follows the clinical specialty classification system, NOT general IT/education categories.

## Primary Categories (专业大类)

| 专业大类 | English Equivalent | When to Use |
|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| 临床医学类 | Clinical Medicine | Most medical AI education competitions (default for medical AI contests) |
| 基础医学类 | Basic Medicine | Research focused on pathology, physiology, molecular biology |
| 公共卫生与预防医学类 | Public Health & Preventive Medicine | Population health, epidemiology, health policy AI tools |
| 中医学类 | Traditional Chinese Medicine | TCM-focused AI applications |
| 口腔医学类 | Stomatology/Dentistry | Dental medicine AI applications |
| 药学类 | Pharmacy | Drug discovery, pharmacology AI tools |
| 护理学类 | Nursing | Nursing education AI tools |
| 医学技术类 | Medical Technology | Medical imaging, lab testing, rehabilitation tech |
| 其他 | Other | Doesn't fit above categories |

## Sub-Category Patterns (细分专业)

When the category is "临床医学类", the 细分专业 typically reflects:

- **AI + clinical specialty**: "临床医学（AI辅助临床科研与教学）"
- **AI + education**: "医学教育技术" or "临床技能训练"
- **Specific disease area**: "心血管" / "神经科学" / "肿瘤" / "眼科" / "儿科" etc.

## Competition Examples

### 全国医学教育智能体大赛 (AI for Medicine)
- **专业大类**: 临床医学类
- **细分专业**: 临床医学（AI辅助临床科研与教学）
- **赛道**: 医学研究支持智能体 / 临床技能训练智能体 / 课程智能体 / 医学科普与健康服务智能体 / 医学教与学辅助智能体 / 医学教育管理智能体 / 其他创新智能体

### 国际数字医学创新大赛
- **专业大类**: 临床医学类
- **细分专业**: 数字医学 / 智能医疗

### 全国大学生医学技术技能大赛
- **专业大类**: 医学技术类
- **细分专业**: 医学检验技术 / 医学影像技术 / 康复治疗技术

## Key Takeaway

For medical education AI competitions in China, ALWAYS default to:
- **专业大类**: 临床医学类
- **细分专业**: 临床医学（AI辅助临床科研与教学）or a more specific variant

Do NOT use "医学信息学", "医学教育技术", "人工智能", "计算机科学与技术" or any non-clinical category. The judging panel is composed of clinical educators, and the taxonomy is designed around clinical specialties.
